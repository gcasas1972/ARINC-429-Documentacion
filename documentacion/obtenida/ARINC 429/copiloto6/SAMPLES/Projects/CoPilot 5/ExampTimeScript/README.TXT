ExampleTimeScript:

Sample project that uses script to convert the time of day (Now) into two
differently formatted ARINC 429 labels after the message is sent on the bus
(signaled by the TimeTag updating):

  hh:mm:ss.s	(hour, minute, second fields)
  hh:mm.m   	(hour, minute fields)

Note: This example was updated for CoPilot 5.30 or later.
